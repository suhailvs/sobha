Sobha Developers Ltd. (SDL) formerly Sobha Developers Private Limited BSE: 532784, NSE: SOBHA is an Indian multinational real estate developer headquartered in Bangalore, India and engaged in the business of construction, development, sale, management and operation of all or any part of townships, housing projects, commercial premises and other related activities. The Company is also engaged in manufacturing activities related to interiors, glazing and metal works and concrete products, which provides backward integration to its turnkey projects. It is primarily focused on residential and contractual projects. The Company executed and handed over 9 residential projects (3.14 million Sq.ft.) and 18 contractual projects (2.08 million Sq.ft.) resulting in an aggregate development of 5.22 million Sq.ft. last year.[4] As of March 31, 2012, the Company has successfully completed 79 residential projects and 209 contractual projects covering an aggregate area of around 51.80 million square feet.[4]

Sobha Developers Limited has the widest backward integration in construction and real estate development business in India that produces almost everything from bricks, interlocking tiles, furniture, interiors, exteriors, glassworks, glazing, to bed mattresses, among many other things that goes into making a comfortable home. Sobha Developers is now a large real estate developer in India with a geographic presence in 22 cities spread across 13 states. Internationally Sobha Developers has significant operations in Oman with a footprint in Dubai
